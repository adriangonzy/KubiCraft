Kubicraft

Easy Minecraft Exporter for Kubity

Download for MacOS Download for Windows Download Jar

Really Easy Minecraft exporting for everyone !

This is an open-source project based on the awesome jmc2obj exporter.

Why exporting to Kubity ?

We believe it's a great way to share your minecraft builds, redstone craftmanship, awesome spawn points or your cute little cottage by the river [check examples above]

Anyone will be able to freely walk on the terrain you exported and feel like in the game directly from their browsers and mobile phones.

For even more awesomeness, Kubity mobile Apps will let you rediscover your exports in Virtual Reality !!

Export in 3 quick steps

Select your map

Select a zone and click Export

Wait for the result in your browser

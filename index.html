Kubicraft

Easy Minecraft Exporter for Kubity

Download for MacOS Download for Windows Download Jar

This project is based on the awesome jmc2obj exporter. However we really want to simplify the usage experience and auto-export to Kubity.

Why Kubity ?

We believe it's a great way to share your minecraft builds, redstone craftmanship, awesome spawn points or you cute little cottage by the river
